Soft Archive is an editorial imprint dedicated to documenting and releasing contemporary craft through a series of curated volumes.

Each volume presents a limited selection of objects by independent artists, chosen for their material integrity, quiet expression, and relevance to the present moment. Objects are released in small editions and, once unavailable, remain as part of the archive.

About

Soft Archive is an independent editorial project focused on contemporary craft.

We work with artists to document and release objects in limited volumes, treating each release as a moment in time — to be collected, used, and remembered. Our approach is archival, not commercial. Each object is photographed, described, and contextualized with the same care given to a museum acquisition.

Let's collaborate

We work with artists to showcase their works and the stories behind them.
